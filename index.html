In-Game Regulations

Michigan State Roleplay Rules

Experience the difference of a community that values quality roleplay, realism, and respect in every scene.

Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Random Deathmatch is highly prohibited. Do not kill someone without having a valid reason.

Random Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Do not ram your vehicle into others for no reason. Random Vehicle Deathmatch is highly prohibited.

Fail Roleplay

Fail RP is an action that your character does that is not possible or realistic within the roleplay environment.

Fear Role-Play

If someone is threatening your life, you must comply with commands given. Do not pull out a weapon or run away.

NIRTP

No Intention to Role Play means constantly breaking in-game rules with no regard for roleplay. This is a bannable offense.

Metagaming

Metagaming is prohibited. Do not use knowledge learned out of character while roleplaying in character.

Respawning

Missing the respawn button is prohibited. Do not respawn in the middle of an active scene. If EMS is in-game, call them.

Crash Roleplay

You must roleplay all crashes. You may only void a crash if it was caused by lag, desync, or happened below 10 mph.

Cop Baiting

Cop baiting is highly prohibited. Do not do unrealistic or dumb activities just to grab law enforcement attention.

Cuff Rushing

You must say -cuffs- before cuffing someone, and you must transport suspects to jail. Auto-jail is not allowed.

Yielding to Staff

You must yield and pull to the side of the road for staff vehicles to pass when required.

New Life Rule (NLR)

Once you pass away, you have forgotten everything from your past life and may not return with prior knowledge.

Unrealistic Avatars

Unrealistic avatars are prohibited. Examples include wings, swords, antlers, tails, and similar unrealistic accessories.

Prohibited Roleplays

Suicide roleplay, terrorist roleplay, bomb roleplay, and alternate agencies such as DHS, DEA, or military roleplay are prohibited.

Common Sense

If something seems like it would not happen in real life, do not do it. Use common sense in all roleplay situations.
